# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in this project! 

Please note that this repository serves as a personal engineering portfolio piece and a static archive of the Buck PMIC and control architecture design. 

**As such, I am not accepting Pull Requests, feature additions, or collaborative modifications at this time.**

You are highly encouraged to fork this repository and modify it for your own use under the terms of the MIT and CERN-OHL-P licenses. 

If you find a critical error in the math or the schematics, feel free to open an Issue to point it out, but please do not submit a PR to fix it.
